Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
         Do’s::
* Think before you post – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. 
* Maintain privacy settings – Regularly review and update your account privacy controls. 
* Share positive and meaningful content – Contribute constructively to online discussions. 
* Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news. 
* Build a professional online presence – Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase skills and achievements. 
Don’ts::
* Don’t share personal or sensitive information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords. 
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or arguments – Stay respectful even during disagreements. 
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content – It can harm your reputation and future opportunities. 
* Don’t overshare your daily life – Excessive posting can compromise your privacy and safety. 
* Don’t trust everything online blindly – Be cautious of scams, fake profiles, and misleading content
